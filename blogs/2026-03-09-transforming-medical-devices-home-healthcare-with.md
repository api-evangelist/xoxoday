Explores how medical device and home healthcare companies can drive adoption and engagement through multi-persona loyalty programs that engage patients, caregivers, clinicians, pharmacists, distributors, and field teams via education-led engagement and performance-linked incentives. Cites the global medical devices market at roughly $570-580 billion in 2024, projected to reach $799-820 billion by 2030, and presents a five-journey engagement framework covering acquisition, activation, engagement, performance, and retention.
